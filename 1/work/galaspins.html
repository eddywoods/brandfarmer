Put Your Spin On It

Gala Spins
Mar 3
Written By Ross Hammond
 

At Gala Spins there’s a game for everyone. With so many fun ways to play, we created ‘What’s Your Spin?’ an integrated cross-channel campaign that encouraged players to show off their quirks, indulgences and wonderfully different approaches to life.

 
 

Working with uber-talented director Fern Beresford we created playful TVCs featuring loveable larger-than-life characters showing us their spin on the games they play.

 
 
 
 
 
 

This was supported by a cross-platform campaign that let everyone do their spin thing including fun brand digital, interactive snapchat filters and a national radio campaign.
